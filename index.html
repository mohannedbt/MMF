MMF: where the limit between Maths and fun is equal to zero
MMF is an android app is combining beautiful animations math videos and quizz in order to make learning maths a fun experience

learn more about our fonctionalities
stunning animations:

our app will contain a multitude of lessons of various topics.in each lesson you'll be introduced in with an amazing animation video built in python with Manim library shout out to 3b1b :) this will help you grasp the main idea of the lesson

Helpful Quizzs:
after each video there will be a quizz with various difficulties which helps you remember all the main ideas of the lessons with an explanation for each correct or false choice
Make a Streak:

each day you visit the app,you mark your day.more days marked ,longer strak achieved .this streak system will make you earger to reopen the app and complete your learning journey
